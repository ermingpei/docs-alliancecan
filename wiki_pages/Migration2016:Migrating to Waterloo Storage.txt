= Getting Access to Files =
To access the new systems please use your Compute Canada credentials. For more information please see [[Migration2016:User_Accounts_and_Groups|this page]].

==Globus Access==
You can transfer files from your personal computer or another Compute Canada system using Globus transfer service using the endpoint '''computecanada#new-endpoint''' as the source or destination. Globus is a fast, easy secure way to transfer files designed for researchers. You can find more information about it on our [[Globus]] page.

To log into the new endpoint please use your Compute Canada username and password. See here to test your password. See here to reset your password.

==SSH Access==
You can also connect directly to the system using [[SSH]] to browse and transfer files by connecting to dtn-sharcnet.computecanada.ca.
